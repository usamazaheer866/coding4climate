About Water Pollution

Water pollution is a pressing global issue, with far-reaching consequences for both human health and the environment. Access to safe and affordable clean drinking water is a fundamental human right, yet millions of people around the world continue to face challenges in obtaining this basic necessity due to water pollution.

Primary Sources of Water Pollution

One of the primary sources of water pollution is industrial discharge, where factories and manufacturing plants release pollutants directly into rivers, lakes, and oceans.

Impact on Human Health

The impact of water pollution on human health is profound, with millions of people suffering from waterborne diseases such as cholera, dysentery, and typhoid each year.

Solutions

Addressing water pollution and ensuring access to safe and affordable clean drinking water requires a multifaceted approach involving government regulation, community engagement, and technological innovation.
